Question No. 5
The monthly salary of Paula = P
The monthly salary of Tom = T

P : T = 5 : 9
T = ⁹/₅ P

T = P + $3200

⁹/₅ P = P + $3200
⁹/₅ P - P = $3200
⁹/₅ P - ⁵/₅ P = $3200
⁴/₅ P = $3200
P = $3200 × ⁵/₄
P = $800 × 5
P = $4.000

a. Simple interest for 4 years = Deposit × interest × time
SI = $3800 × 3% × 4
SI = $38 × 12
SI = $456

b. If SI = $171, time of Deposit = ?
$171 = $3800 × 3% × t
$171 = $114 × t
t = $171 ÷ $114
t = 1,5 ...........The time of deposit is 1,5 years
