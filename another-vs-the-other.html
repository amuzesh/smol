Jawaban dari "another vs the other​"

Jika anda sedang mencari jawaban dari soal another vs the other​ ente berada di tempat yang tepat. Kami mempunyai 1 jawaban mengenai another vs the other​. Silakan baca jawaban lebih lanjut di bawah:

Jawaban: #1:

Jawaban:

a) Another one is a chicken.

b) The other bird is a crow.

Penjelasan:

Menggunakan kata The other dengan singular nouns berarti yang kedua dari dua hal (the second of two things) atau orang, dan atau kebalikan dari satu dua set kata.

Example sentence of "the other"

- In regard to this question, history stands to the other sciences as experimental science stands to abstract science.

- I prefer to read this book all day than the other book i've read in the bookstore.

The other menjadi kata pronoun.

Kita bisa menggunakan kata the other menjadi sebuah kata pronoun,  terutama untuk melihat kembali ke sesuatu yang telah disebutkan dalam kalimat:

- She had her dolls in one hand and a bunch of flowers in the other.

- He has two turtles, one is small and the other is quite big.

Menggunakan kata Another dengan singular nouns:

- I don’t like this library, its scary and eerie. Is there another library around here we could go to?

- You've seen my cat, but i have another pet you haven't seen it before.
