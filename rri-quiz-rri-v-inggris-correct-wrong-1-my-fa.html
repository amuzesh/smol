Jawaban: #1:

THE ORDER OF ADJECTIVE

1) My father brought back a (beautiful-old-wooden) desk ✅

2. They prepare a bowl of a (delicious-traditional-Italian-spiral) pasta for dinner. ❌

➡ delicious spiral traditional Italian

3) They have decided to buy the (red-metallic-Honda-luxurious) car. ❌

➡ luxurious red metallic Honda

4. He is a (young-handsome-Irish) man in our office. ❌

➡ handsome young Irish

5. We own (several-valuable-watercolor-impressionist) paintings. ✅

EXPLANATION

Five sentences above are categorized in the noun phrase. A noun phrase contains a head noun and more modifiers.

There's a spesific order (the order of adjective) to form the noun phrase that contains more than one adjective before a noun. These adjectives work together to modify their noun.

We must know which adjective comes first and which one comes later.

It starts from the left side to the head noun;determiner, opinion, size, shape, age, color, origin, material, and purpose.

1) Determiner ➡ it conveys the noun is singular or plural, definite or indefinite.

  • Exp  : a, an, the, several, that, some, a pair of, two, my

2) Opinion ➡ it conveys an evaluation about something/somebody by using our thoughts/feelings.

  • Exp  : beautiful, silly, hard, difficult, easy, intelligent, sweet, exciting, expensive, cute, favorite, interesting, delicious, handsome, luxurious, valuable.

3) Size [Dimension] ➡ an objective information how big/small something is.

  • Exp : small, large, big, long, short, tall, enormous.

4) Physical Quality ➡ an objective information about the texture.

  • Exp : soft, rough, smooth.

5) Shape ➡ an objective information about the shape of something.

  • Exp  : round, triangle, square, circle, spiral.

6) Age ➡ an objective information how old something/someone is.

  • Exp : old, young, ancient, middle-aged, new, antique, classic, traditional.

7) Color ➡ an objective information about the colour of something.

  • Exp : purple, yellow, black, blue, red, silver, white.

8) Origin/Nationality ➡ where something/someone is from.

  • Exp : Indonesian, French, Spanish, American, Chinese, Malaysian, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Persian.

9) Material ➡ what it is made of.

  • Exp  : cotton, paper, metal, rock, sand, cloth, plastic, leather, wooden, metallic.

10) Purpose ➡ what it is used for.

  • Exp : cooking, roasting, racing, dancing.

11) Noun/Head ➡ a person/thing that is being described.

  • Exp : table, chair, book, cat, car, girl, hair, desk, woman, shirt, actor, cupboard, van, jar.
