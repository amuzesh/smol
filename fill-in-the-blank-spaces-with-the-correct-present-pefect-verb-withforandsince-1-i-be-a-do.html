Berikut jawaban dengan kalimat yang lengkap:

1. I have been a doctor since 2015.

2. We have lived here for a long time.

3. I have been seen Jamie since Tuesday.

4. Lisa has been at work since 6 o'clock this morning.

5. Emily and Anton have been friends for three years.

6. We have knew each other since we were children.

7. Dave have been a teacher since 1997.

8. The animals have not ate for three days.

9. Sam have been in Paris since Wednesday.

10. Mrs. Smith has owned that house for a long time.

Penjelasan:

Present perfect tense digunakan untuk menyatakan kegiatan yang dimulai pada masa lalu dan masih dilakukan atau masih berhubungan dengan masa sekarang.

Pattern untuk present perfect tense adalah:

Subject + Have/Has + Verb 3

Catatan:

Have digunakan untuk subject I, You, We, They

Has digunakan untuk subject He, She, It
