daughters Bawang Putih, who was her stepdaughter and Bawang Merah a daughter of her
Mak Dadapan did not treat the girls in the same way to her own daughter she was very to
Bawang Merah could do and get everything she wanted and did not like helping her mother
the household To Bawang Putih, her stepmother was very severe. This girl had to do everything
in the household
One day, Bawang Putih had to wash clothes at the river She
was tired and did not notice that a piece of clothing was dragged
by the stream The stepmother was furious and hit Bawang Putih
with a piece of stick. Then she let her look for the piece of clothing
at the riverbank
Bawang Putih returned to the river and followed the stream. She
met a man who was bathing his horse on the riverside
"Excuse me, Sir Have you seen a piece of clothing dragged by
the stream?
"No, I haven't Just ask the man who is fishing downstream
Then Bawang Putih walked away. She saw an old woman who was fishing on the riverside
"Excuse me, Ma'am. Did you see a piece of clothing?"
"Yes, my dear. Come with me I'll give it to you."
Who was in fact the old woman? She was a demon, called Nini Buto ljo. On arriving at Nini
Buto ljo's house, Bawang Putih was frightened because she was in the demon's house Any utensif
was different from which she used at home. The ladle was made of human hands, the mugs of
a human skull; wood for cooking was human bones.
After cooking and cleaning the house, "Ma'am, everything is clear. May I get my piece of
dothing and go home?"
"Well
, it is already dark. You will meet my brother on your way and he will eat you up Thus,
stay here tonight and return home tomorrow."
The next moming, Nini Buto ljo gave her a piece of clothing and a melon. Bawang Putih
walked home as quickly as she could. On arriving at home, the stepmother took a piece of
dothing and melon from Bawang Putih's hands. Quickly she opened the melon. Wow! Gold,
silver, and precious stones!
Bawang Merah wanted to get the same melon as Bawang Putih's. She looked for Nini Buto
Tjohouse She found and met Nini Buto ljo but she refused to help her clean her house. After a
while, she asked permission to get back home Nini Buto ljo gave her the melontoo She went
home in a hurry and opened as soon as she got home. Instead of gold, silver, and precious
stones, that melon was filled with insects and crawling animals, snakes, scorpions, and other
creepy animals. Bawang Merah and Mak Dadapan, who were greedy, ran away in a great fear

Sinopsis cerita:

Mak Dadapan adalah seorang janda yang hidup dengan anak kandungnya yaitu Bawang Merah dan anak tirinya yakni Bawang Putih. Dia memperlakukan Bawang Putih dengan tidak adil. Bawang Putih yang mengerjakan semua pekerjaan rumah tangga. Suatu hari ketika mencuci baju di sungai, Bawang Putih tidak menyadari sepotong pakaian terseret arus. Ketika Bawang Putih mencari baju itu oleh perintah ibu tirinya, dia bertanya kepada Buto Ijo. Buto Ijo meminta Bawang Putih ke rumahnya, dan membersihkan rumahnya. Besoknya Buto Ijo memberikan pakaian itu dan sebuah melon untuk Bawang Putih. Setiba di rumah, ibu tirinya membukanya dan melihat emas, perak, dan batu berharga. Bawang Merah pun ingin mendapat barang berharga seperti itu, dia pergi ke rumah Buto Ijo. Namun, dia tidak mau membersihkan rumah Buto Ijo, dan segera berpamitan pulang. Bawang Merah diberi melon oleh Buto Ijo dan setelah dibuka, didapatinya serangga, ular, kalajengking, dan lain-lain. Mak Dadapan dan Bawang Merah yang serakah lari ketakutan.
