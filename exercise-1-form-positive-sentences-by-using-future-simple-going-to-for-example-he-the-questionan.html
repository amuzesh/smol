PEMBAHASAN

✎ Simple Present Future Tense

☛ Digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu pekerjaan yang akan dilakukan di masa yang akan datang.

Simple Present Future Tense dengan Modal "Will"

☛ Pola Simple Present Future Tense

  • (+) subject + will + verb1 + object + complement
  • (-) subject + will + not + verb1 + object + complement
  • (?) Will + subject + verb1 + object + complement

☛ Catatan

  • Will digunakan untuk semua subjek.

Simple Present Future Tense dengan "be going to"

☛ Pola Simple Present Future Tense

  • (+) subject + to be + going to + verb1 + object + complement
  • (-) subject + be + not + going to + verb1 + object + complement
  • (?) be + subject + going to + verb1 + object + complement

☛ Pengelompokan Subject berdasarkan To be

To Be Present

  • am : I
  • is : He, She, It, dan Nama Orang Tunggal (Misal: Rini)
  • are : You, We, They, dan Nama Orang Banyak (Misal: Rini dan Rina)

CONTOH KALIMAT

1. Saya akan pergi ke pasar

  • I will go to market
  • I am going to go to market

2. Dia akan mengunjungi rumah neneknya

  • He will visit his grandmother's house
  • He is going to visit his grandmother's house

3. Abraham dan Saya akan bermain badminton

  • Abraham and I will play badminton
  • Abraham and I are going to play badminton

4. Kucing itu akan minum susunya

  • The cat will drink its milk
  • The cat is going to drink its milk
