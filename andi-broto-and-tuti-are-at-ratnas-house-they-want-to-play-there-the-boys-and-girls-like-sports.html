badminton?
Broto: I do. But I don't have my racket here.
Andi: Where is your racket?
Broto: At home?
Andi: Who has a racket for broto? Do you have one, Ratna?
Ratna:Yes,I do. Do you want to use my racket, Broto?

to play?
Ratna: No, I don't want
to play badminton now. I prefer to play pingpong with tuti.
