Thave a pet. It is a dog. My sister gave it to me last year. I name
it Dodon. It is quite big. He is around 60 cm tall. He is also very
heavy. He weighs around 30 kg. He has straight fur. Its colour is
creamy gold. It is dense and water-repellent. It means water cannot
be absorbed or passing through the fur.
Although he is big, Dodon is very cute. He has dark eyes. His
eyes are widely apart. He has small ears. His skull is broad and his
neck is muscular. His forelegs are straight while his hind legs are
muscular
Dodon is very active and cheerful. When he is happy, he wags his straight tail He often uses
his strong legs to run here and there and jump cheerfully.

I have a pet. It is a dog. My sister gave it to me last year. (aku punya hewan peliharaan. Itu adalah guguk. Adikku memberikannya kepadaku tahun lalu.)

I name it Dodon. (Saya menamainya Dodol)

Arti name disini adalah "menamai" bukan "nama".

Objek It adalah si guguk itu.

It is quite big. (Cukup besar)

He is around 60 cm tall. (Tingginya sekitar 60 cm)

He is also very heavy. (Dia juga sangat berat)

He weighs around 30 kg. (Beratnya sekitar 30 kg.)

He has straight fur. (Dia memiliki bulu yang lurus)
