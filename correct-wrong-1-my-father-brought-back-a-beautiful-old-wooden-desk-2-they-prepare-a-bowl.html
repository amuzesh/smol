Jawaban: #2:

THE ORDER OF ADJECTIVE

1) My father brought back a (beautiful-old-wooden) desk ✅

2. They prepare a bowl of a (delicious-traditional-Italian-spiral) pasta for dinner. ❌

➡ delicious spiral traditional Italian

3) They have decided to buy the (red-metallic-Honda-luxurious) car. ❌

➡ luxurious red metallic Honda

4. He is a (young-handsome-Irish) man in our office. ❌

➡ handsome young Irish

5. We own (several-valuable-watercolor-impressionist) paintings. ✅

EXPLANATION

Five sentences above are categorized in the noun phrase. A noun phrase contains a head noun and more modifiers.

There's a tight rule (the order of adjective) to form the noun phrase that contains more than one adjective.

It starts from the left side to the head noun;determiner, opinion, size, shape, age, color, origin, material and purpose.

1) Determiner [Amount]

Exp  : a, an, the, several, that, some, a pair of, two, my and so on.

2) Opinion

Exp  : beautiful, silly, hard, difficult, easy, enormous, intelligent, sweet, exciting, best, expensive, cute, favorite, talented, interesting, smart, delicious, handsome, luxurious, valuable.

3) Size [Dimension]

Exp : small, large, big, long, short, tall

4) Physical Quality

Exp : soft, rough, thin

5) Shape

Exp  : round, triangle, square, circle, spiral.

6) Age/Temperature

Exp : old, young, ancient, middle-aged, new, antique, classic, traditional, warm, cold

7) Color

Exp : purple, yellow, black, blue, red, silver, white.

8) Origin/Nationality

Exp : Indonesian, French, Spanish, American, Chinese, Malaysian, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Persian, Irish.

9) Material ➡ what it is made of.

Exp  : cotton, paper, metal, rock, sand, cloth, plastic, leather, wooden, metallic.

10) Purpose/Qualifier ➡ what it is used for.

Exp : cooking, roasting, racing, dancing, cough.

11) Noun/Head ➡ before modifying

Exp : table, chair, book, cat, car, girl, hair, cup, desk, woman, container, shirt, actor, cupboard, van, jar, wooden.
