It was a bright beautiful wednesday,when stephani Eller decided to go hiking in one of the forest near her city , she went on walking and tried to find a way back ,but she failed.
Eller had to struggle by herself to survive in the Hawaiian wilderness.she went up and down the bushy hills in the heat of the weather .Eller ate wild raspberries , stawberries , guavas ,and even a number of moths to sustain herself.To make sure she had enough water, Eller tried to be constantly
near water source , as a river or a waterfall.
Throughout her 17 days in the forest ,Eller heard about 20 helicopters flying over her . The helicopters belonged to the rescue teams ,but they failed to see her .
Finally ,on a fateful Friday afternoon one of the rescue team helicopters spotted her . At that point Eller was barefoot, her getting carried away downstream as she was drying them out overnight.
While Eller had a leg fracture ,abrasions and severe sumburn , she was in relatively good condition and is currently on the path to recovery.
