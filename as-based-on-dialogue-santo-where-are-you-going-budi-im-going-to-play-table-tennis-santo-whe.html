Jika anda sedang melihat jawaban mengenai soal as based on dialogue
Santo : where are you going?
Budi : I'm going to play table tennis.
Santo : Where do you usually play it?
Budi : At Kusuma court. It's near here.
Santo : How many people are there?
Budi : There are just eight boys including the coach.
Santo : That must be fun.
Budi : What are you doing in the Veranda?
Santo : I'm just sitting. I have nothing to do.
Budi : Let's play table tennis together!
Santo : Okay. But I don't have a bat. May I borrow your yours?
Budi : Sure. I bring a pairs.
Santo : Okay. Let's go now!
Budi : Wait! Put your shoes on! you don't want to go there barefoot, do you?
Santo : (Laughing) You right.
1. What does Budi invite Santo for?
2. What does Santo borrow from Budi?
3. Find the expressions of instruction!
4. Do they play tennis together?
