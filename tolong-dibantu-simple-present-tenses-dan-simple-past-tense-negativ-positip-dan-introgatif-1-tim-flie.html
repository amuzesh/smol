Jawaban: #1:

Simple present, untuk menyatakan fakta, hal yang terjadi rutin

  • positive (+) : S + v1 + s/es
  • negative (-) : S + do/does not + v1
  • interrogative (?) : do/does + S + v1?
  • catatan: I/you/they/we atau subjek plural pakai do. He/she/it atau nama orang pakai does, verb nya ditambahkan huruf s atau es.
  1. (+) tim flies his kite. (-) tim doesn't fly his kite. (?)does tim fly his kite?
  2. (+) vanessa works on her essay . (-) vanessa doesn't work on her essay. (?) does vanessa work on her essay?
  3. (+) kevin and niko live in a house . (-) kevin and niko don't live in a house. (?) do kevin and niko live in a house?
  4. (+) justin understands Russian . (-) justin doesn't understand Russian. (?) does justin understand Russian?
  5. (+) our cat catches mice . (-) our cat doesn't catch mice. (?) does our cat catch mice?
  6. (+) I have two dogs . (-) I don't have two dogs. (?) do I/you have two dogs?
  7. (+) the dogs bark. (-) the dogs don't bark. (?) do the dogs bark?

Simple past, untuk menyatakan kejadian yang sudah berlalu

  • positive (+) : S + v2
  • negative (-) : S + did not + v1
  • interrogative (?) : did + S + v1?
  • catatan, hati-hati bentuk verb 2 yang irregular, seperti bring->brought, teach->taught, hide->hid. bentuk verb2 regular biasanya v1 + d/ed.
  1. (+) tim flew his kite. (-) tim didn't fly his kite. (?)did tim fly his kite?
  2. (+) vanessa worked on her essay . (-) vanessa didn't work on her essay. (?) did vanessa work on her essay?
  3. (+) kevin and niko lived in a house . (-) kevin and niko didn't live in a house. (?) did kevin and niko live in a house?
  4. (+) justin understood Russian . (-) justin didn't understand Russian. (?) did justin understand Russian?
  5. (+) our cat caught mice . (-) our cat didn't catch mice. (?) did our cat catch mice?
  6. (+) I had two dogs . (-) I didn't have two dogs. (?) did I/you have two dogs?
  7. (+) the dogs barked. (-) the dogs didn't bark. (?) did the dogs bark?
