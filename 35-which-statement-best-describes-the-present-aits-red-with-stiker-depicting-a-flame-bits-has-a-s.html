Jawaban: #1:

UNDERSTANING CONTEXT

Jawaban:

33. What is the text about?

a. The writer's birthday celebration

b. The procedure how to wear a helmet perfectly

c. The general description of helmets

d. The description of the writer's helmet

Answer: jawaban yang benar adalah d. Dari teks tersebut pada paragraph kedua, writer mendeskripsikan helm yang diterima dari orang tuanya sebagai hadiah ulang tahun.

34. How does the writer usually go to school?

a. He walks with his friends

b. He takes a bus

c. He rides his bicycle

d. His father drives him to school

Answer: Jawaban yang benar adalah c. Hal ini secara jelas dinyatakan pada kalimat terakhir paragraph 1, "My parents gave it because I always go to school by bicycle."

35. Which statement best describes the present  

a.It's red with sticker depicting a flame

b.It has a silver strap and orange buckle

c.It has big holes at the back  

d.There is a sticker saying 'keep my head'

Answer: jawaban yang benar adalah b. Dari keempat point jawaban diatas, deskripsi yang sesuai dengan teks adalah point b. It has a silver strap and orange buckle (kalimat kedua paragraph 2)

36.what is the function of the black buckles at the back of the helmet?

a.to keep the helmet stable

b.for air circulation  

c.to make the helmet attractive  

d.to make the helmet stronger and safer

Answer: Jawaban yang benar adalah a. Hal ini dinyatakan dalam kalimat ketiga paragraph 2: There are two black buckles at the back to keep the helmet stable.
