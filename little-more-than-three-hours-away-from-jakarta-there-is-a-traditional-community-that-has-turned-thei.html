Jawaban: #1:

Jawaban:

Gerund sentence: Living in voluntary isolation, they are a secretive group who turn away outsiders.

Active sentence:

They don’t own phones, use the Internet, wear shoes or listen to the radio.

Passive sentence:

Generally, the Baduy are divided into two groups: the Baduy Dalam and the Baduy Luar.

Active and passive in a sentence:

This group lives close to the city of Banten (active) and they are known as the Baduy people (passive).

Penjelasan:

Gerund berpola Verb + ing yang membentuk kata benda (noun) yang berfungsi sebagai subjek atau objek.

Active sentences berpola subject + verb.

Passive sentence berpola object + auxiliary verb + verb 3 + by subject.
