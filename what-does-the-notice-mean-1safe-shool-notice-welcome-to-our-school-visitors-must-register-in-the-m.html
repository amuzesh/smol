Jawaban: #1:

What does the notice mean?

1. Safe school notice. Welcome to our school. Visitors must register in the main office. Drugs and weapons prohibited. No trespassing .

ANSWER: The notice tells us that we are welcomed to the school. Every visitor has to register in the main office. Visitors are not allowed to bring drugs and weapons. It is not allowed to enter without permission.

2. Health notice. No pets on school grounds .

ANSWER: We are not allowed to bring any pets on the school grounds.

3. Safe school zone. This area is protected by video surveillance .

ANSWER: It tells us that anything happens are recorded by the closed-circuit television (CCTV).

4. Visitors, welcome to our school. Please use main entrance and register at office.

ANSWER: It tells us that visitors are welcomed tho the school. Visitors have to pass through main entrance and register at the office.

Pembahasan

Notice adalah salah satu short functional text yang berisi informasi (information), pengumuman (announcement), petunjuk (instruction) atau peringatan (warnings).

Soal di atas merupakan notice yang berisi informasi dan petunjuk.
